FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Can I generate reports for any branch of my project?
Yes, the plugin allows you to select any branch of your SonarQube project and generate an up-to-date report instantly.

What is the difference between PDF and ODT reports?
PDFs are ideal for sharing final results with your team or clients, while ODTs allow you to edit the document and add comments, conclusions, or audit data.

Do the reports include code coverage and duplication information?
Yes, reports can include metrics such as coverage, duplications, bugs, vulnerabilities, and code smells, among others.

Is it compatible with all SonarQube™ editions (Community, Developer, Enterprise)?
Yes, the plugin works with all editions, although some advanced metrics may depend on the edition of SonarQube™ you are using.

Can I include my company logo or name in the reports?
Yes, you can customise reports with your logo on the footer or cover page, and adjust styles in ODT templates.
