We’ve just released a new version of **bitegarden Report for SonarQube&trade; Cloud**, featuring improvements that make reports more informative and fully compatible with the new MQR Mode.

<h2>What’s new in this version?</h2>

The key update is support for **MQR Mode in SonarQube&trade; Cloud**. This means you can now generate reports aligned with this approach, using the new severity levels Blocker, High, Medium, Low, and Info. In addition, we’ve introduced new sections that display evidence organized by quality characteristics.

<h2>How do you update?</h2>

You can **download the new version of Report for SonarQube&trade; Cloud** through [this link](https://marketplace.bitegarden.com/download/download-form/64).
