Keeping a SonarQube instance optimized is essential for the performance and clarity of your analysis. In the new version of **Control Center for SonarQube&trade; Server 1.8**, we’ve introduced two key features designed to help you identify and eliminate “noise” from your instance: the new Housekeeping section and advanced filters in Project Management. Below, we detail how these improvements will make your daily work easier.

<h2>New Housekeeping section</h2>

The main highlight of this release is the **introduction of a section called Housekeeping**, designed to detect and manage empty projects. From here you can:

- View empty projects in a centralized way. <br>

- Identify which projects are not providing any data. <br>

- Prepare their removal in a controlled manner. <br>

<img width="100%" src="/img/sonarqube-control-center/control-center-sonarqube-housekeeping.png" alt="Control Center SonarQube Housekeeping">
<br>

<h2>New intelligent filtering in the projects screen</h2>

The projects view also evolves with two key capabilities:

**Empty project filter**: From the Project Management section, you can now directly filter and find projects that contain no data, avoiding unnecessary manual exploration.

<img width="100%" src="/img/sonarqube-control-center/control-center-sonarqube-project-management-empty-projects.png" alt="Control Center SonarQube Empty projects">
<br>

**Branch search**: A dedicated search tool has been added to locate projects based on a specific branch, enabling you to:

- Detect whether a branch contains data or is empty. <br>

- Analyze real activity per branch. <br>

- Identify inconsistencies across environments or workflows. <br>

If you have the [Universal Plugin Manager for SonarQube&trade; Server (UPM)](https://marketplace.bitegarden.com/product/bitegardenUniversalPluginManager) you simply need to click the update button in your instance administration. If you haven’t tried Control Center for SonarQube&trade; Server yet, you can download it and test it in your instance from [this link](https://marketplace.bitegarden.com/product/bitegardenControlCenter).
