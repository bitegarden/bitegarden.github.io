Why use Control Center for SonarQube™?

Control Center for SonarQube™ allows administrators to search projects by a key or name, by scan date, by scan pending, by public, by visibility (public, private or both).

Thanks to this puglin it will be easier for administrators to view the basic data (total lines of code, by language, etc.) of the selected projects to be able to make management decisions and manage the instance in a faster way.

This plugin answers, for example, the following questions: Are there any old projects that are no longer analyzed and can be deleted? What are the most active projects?

Once the projects are filtered, administrators can apply actions such as a bulk tagging of selected projects so that they can then be easily found in any section of SonarQube.

Mass management of users in SonarQube

With the Control Center plugin for SonarQube™ you can also manage users in bulk.

From the dedicated section Control Center - User Management, you can view all users with access to the instance and you will be able to modify the identity provider they have configured. This management, you can do it individually or in bulk.







Bulk Hotspot Management in SonarQube

With the Control Center for SonarQube plugin, you can also manage hotspots in bulk that have been resolved but not yet commented on.

From the dedicated section Control Center - Hotspot Management, you can view all the hotspots in the instance that have been resolved, either as SAFE, FIXED, or ACKNOWLEDGED, but that have not yet had any comments added.
Additionally, you can add comments to all the selected hotspots.
